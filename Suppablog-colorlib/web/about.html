About Anything Goes

So... Out of curiosity and boredom from the COVID-19 lockdown, I tried my hands in cryptocurrency last March 2020. I instantly got hooked. It was new to me and it was exciting and interesting playing with these magic internet money. There were also a lot of amazing and beautiful crypto wallet apps, which, as an app developer myself, really impressed me. I ended up wallet-hopping for days, trying out every crypto wallet app I could find. I finally settled with Trust Wallet as my main non-custodial wallet and the Crypto.com App as my custodial wallet and main crypto investment and spending platform.

Crypto.com hosts a lot of events where you can participate to earn crypto or buy stuff at huge discounts. One time, they partnered with Unstoppable Domains where you can buy your own decentralized internet domain and get a 10% cashback (!!!) if you buy using their native $CRO token (Crypto.com's own magic internet money). Of course, I bought my own domain rexmtorres.crypto.


So now, I have this blogging website, which, I'm not yet sure what to put in. This is my very first attempt at blogging. So, anything goes...
